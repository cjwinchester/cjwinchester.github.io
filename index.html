aphis-animal-facilities-2019.csvNEW!
National database of USDA-regulated animal research facilities (anyone who submits an APHIS Form 7023) as of July 2019. Original FOIA work by the good folks over at The Government Attic -- I just ripped the PDF tables into a CSV.

co-concealed-weapons-permits.csv
Colorado concealed handgun permitting data, 2003-2017 (notes).

mlb-walkup-music.csv
Walkup music for Major League Baseball players (via).

The Onion: American Voices 💙
A Google Sheet with data collected from The Onion's "American Voices" series, 1996-08-07 to present. Updated regularly.

ppld-challenges.csv
Pikes Peak Library District materials challenged by a member of the public, 2001-2019 (notes). Updated annually.

meatball-surgery.csv
Every time the phrase "meatball surgery" was spoken during an episode of the hit TV show M*A*S*H.

hotdog-contest.csv
Nathan's Famous Hot Dog Eating Contest results, 2002-2019. Updated annually.

tx-death-row-media-list.csv
A list of journalists who witness executions in Texas (scraper). Updated whenever there's an execution.

animal-count-nouns.csv
A tidy CSV from a Wikipedia table listing count nouns for animals.

co-cannabis-sales.csv
County-level cannabis sales in Colorado, 2014-present (see this notebook). Updated monthly.

scrabble.csv
Scrabble tile frequency and values.
