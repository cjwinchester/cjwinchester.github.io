Records request generator

Making it easier to file open records requests with local government in the Omaha metro area

Instructions

Fill out the form and click Submit. Select the text that appears. Copy into your email or letter. Any information you enter is generated entirely within your browser. Nothing is kept or shared with anyone.

Tips

Do your research. Make sure the information isn't already available elsewhere. Before filing a request, contact the agency to understand how the records are kept.
